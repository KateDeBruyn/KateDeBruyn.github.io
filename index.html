About

Currently studying (surviving) a digital arts degree at The University of Witwatersrand in South Africa. Not only am I developing the will to live but the occasional game here and there too. And this website.

Game designing, programming, developing, mistaking, laughing, crying and LOTS of playing has become my everyday life. I couldn't imagine doing anything else and for that I am grateful.

Graphic to Game Design

I spent a year studying graphic design at the University of Johannesburg and set up a portfolio on Behance. This includes some projects that assessed our skills and abilities, ranging from communication design to photography. My passionate for digital design finally got put to work when I moved back to Joburg in 2019 and began studying.

Pastimes

I started streaming on Twitch this year. I'm a horror games enthusiast but a sucker for the entire Sims series. No, fortunately that is not me in the VR headset. Mental health > VR horror games.

I also have spent many years making and painting ceramics as a hobby. That work can be viewed here at Hardcore Ceramics.
