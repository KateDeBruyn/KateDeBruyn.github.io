Sims 4 Activism Mod

Currently working on an activist mod for the Sims 4 where players can make their Sims fight for causes they believe in. So far I have been working on the underlying trait that the sims needs to have in order to play out activist scenarios - talking about socio-political issues or causes, trying to change an obstructionist's view, acting better than them and so on. Covet Fashion Mod

Before starting on the Sims mod, I tried out a mod for another game, Covet Fashion. I wanted to make a sell system where the player could sell items of clothing they did not want anymore. This one was a of a flop in terms of its actual value in the game, but I was really happy with how far my coding skills came in this project.

Heartsherf

Heartsherf was inpsired by Arrietty and was worked on by Jessica Hon and I last year. The player plays as Bo, a miniature human who is always accompanied by a friendly bee. Bo begins in the stores front and will first come across the granny NPC. Talking to the granny will give the player a chance to obtain an object from the granny that will help Bo when meeting the second NPC, Alex, in the courtyard. Being a good listener will help you with the granny and being sympathetic and generous will help you with Alex. Jess worked on the artwork side of the project while I worked on the coding and programming.
